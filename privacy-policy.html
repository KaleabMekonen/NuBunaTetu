NuBunaTetu ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our content scheduling and management application ("the App"), which connects to TikTok via the TikTok API.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize NuBunaTetu to connect to your TikTok account, we receive your TikTok username, display name, and profile information as permitted by TikTok's API.
  • Content Data: Videos, captions, hashtags, and scheduling preferences you create or approve within the App.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including features accessed and actions taken.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as operating system type and version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and manage your authorized session
  • Upload and publish videos to TikTok on your behalf, only after your explicit approval
  • Generate content suggestions based on trending topics relevant to your audience
  • Improve the performance and features of the App
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Human Review Requirement

NuBunaTetu does not publish any content without explicit human review and approval. All AI-generated scripts, videos, and captions must be reviewed and approved by the account holder before any posting action is taken via the TikTok API.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • TikTok Platform: Content and metadata are shared with TikTok as necessary to fulfill posting requests you have approved.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information where required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., cloud infrastructure) strictly for operating the App. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. TikTok API Data Usage

NuBunaTetu uses TikTok's Content Posting API solely to upload and publish videos that you have reviewed and approved. We comply with TikTok's Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We do not access, store, or use TikTok user data beyond what is necessary to operate the App.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

NuBunaTetu is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also revoke NuBunaTetu's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings under "Manage App Permissions."

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
